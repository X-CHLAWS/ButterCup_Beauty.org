Frequently Asked Questions

Where are your products made?

All Buttercup Essentials products are proudly made in Ghana using high-quality ingredients and expert formulations.

Are your products FDA approved?

Yes, all our cosmetic products are FDA approved, ensuring they meet strict quality and safety standards.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile money, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by location. Within Accra, delivery typically takes 1-2 business days. Other regions may take 3-5 business days.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only ship within Ghana. We're working on expanding our shipping options in the future.
